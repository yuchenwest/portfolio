Intro 介绍

For xAI's Multilingual Audio AI Tutor role, I bring a rare combination for Chinese-language audio work: native Mandarin paired with native-level Shanghainese, plus lifelong exposure to the Gan-speaking region of Shangrao, Jiangxi, where I was born.

Beyond fluency, I have a trained ear for accent variation — I can identify Chinese regional accents across the country, and distinguish English varieties from speakers of different national backgrounds. I also have a natural ability to reproduce these accents, which I demonstrate in the dubbing samples below.

Professionally, I spend my days on high-volume document review as an Immigration Case Manager: ~46 packets/month, <2% QA exceptions annually, 24-hour first-response SLA. That precision discipline is what I'd bring to audio annotation work.
